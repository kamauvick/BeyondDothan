We are a church located in the heart of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. My wife Teri and I have been rooted in this commu nity since our childhood and that spurred my passion to plant a church here. It is our prayer to see the residents of this neig hborhood worshipping Jesus Christ through the biblical, Christ-centered messages preached, the songs we sing that glorify Christ and in our fellowship with others. Our mission is rooted in the Gospel power to transform lives by restoring the relationship of men and women with their Creator, and as a result of that, with one another. We are a simple church that neither adds to nor subtracts from th e gospel, for it is the gospel itself which is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes. Our prayer is to build a community of believers that serves Christ and others using the gifts and talents God has given us.
